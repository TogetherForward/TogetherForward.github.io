The inspiration behind the T-shirt’s design was the Badger spirit. The team members, both being exchange students at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, had never seen anything like the love for school by its students before. As part of their project in the course MHR322: Introduction to Entrepreneurial Management by Professor Dan Olszewski, they decided to create a design which expressed much but was minimal in its expression.

What the design infers is the spirit of giving back that has been inculcated in UW-Madison alumni. Together Forward* is the tagline of Wisconsin School of Business (WSB). Our target customers are students and alumni of UW-Madison’s WSB. All profits generated from this project will proceed to the Great People Scholarship which works on supporting scholars who would otherwise be unable to attend the university due to financial reasons.
